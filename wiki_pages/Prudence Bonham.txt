Prudence Bonham (born 1948) is an Australian former politician and marine biologist. She was a Hobart City Council Alderman from 1990 to 2002 and the Deputy Lord Mayor of Hobart from 1994 to 2002.Bonham spent 26 years working as a marine biologist for CSIRO. She took part in various marine expeditions in the Australian region and the Antarctic.Bonham was inducted into the Tasmanian Honour Roll of Women in 2013.
